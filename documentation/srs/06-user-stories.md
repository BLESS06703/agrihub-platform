# 6. User Stories

## Epic 1: Onboarding & Account Management

### US-001: Account Creation
As a farmer, I want to create an account with my email and phone number,
so that I can access the platform.
- Points: 5 | Priority: CRITICAL

### US-002: Email Verification
As a new user, I want to verify my email with a code,
so that my account is secured and activated.
- Points: 3 | Priority: CRITICAL

### US-003: Tenant Registration
As an organization admin, I want to register my organization as a tenant,
so that my team can work in an isolated workspace.
- Points: 8 | Priority: CRITICAL

### US-004: User Invitation
As a tenant owner, I want to invite team members by email,
so that they can join my organization with assigned roles.
- Points: 5 | Priority: HIGH

### US-005: Role Assignment
As a tenant owner, I want to assign roles to users,
so that each person has appropriate access permissions.
- Points: 5 | Priority: HIGH

### US-006: Language Selection
As a farmer, I want to switch between English and Chichewa,
so that I can use the app in my preferred language.
- Points: 8 | Priority: HIGH

### US-007: Password Reset
As a user, I want to reset my forgotten password via email,
so that I can regain access to my account.
- Points: 3 | Priority: HIGH

### US-008: Profile Management
As a user, I want to update my profile information,
so that my details stay current.
- Points: 2 | Priority: MEDIUM

## Epic 2: Farm Management

### US-009: Farm Registration
As a farmer, I want to register my farm with GPS location,
so that my farm is digitally recorded with accurate coordinates.
- Points: 8 | Priority: CRITICAL

### US-010: Field Creation
As a farmer, I want to create fields within my farm,
so that I can manage different growing areas separately.
- Points: 5 | Priority: CRITICAL

### US-011: GPS Farm Mapping
As a farmer, I want to walk my farm perimeter to capture boundaries,
so that my farm area is accurately measured.
- Points: 13 | Priority: HIGH

### US-012: Crop Catalog Browsing
As a farmer, I want to browse the crop catalog with recommendations,
so that I can choose the best crops for my farm.
- Points: 5 | Priority: HIGH

### US-013: Planting Recording
As a farmer, I want to record when and what I plant in each field,
so that I have a digital planting history.
- Points: 5 | Priority: CRITICAL

### US-014: Growth Stage Tracking
As a farmer, I want to track my crop through growth stages,
so that I know what actions to take at each stage.
- Points: 8 | Priority: MEDIUM

### US-015: Input Application Recording
As a farmer, I want to record fertilizer and chemical applications,
so that I know what inputs were used and when.
- Points: 5 | Priority: HIGH

### US-016: Harvest Recording
As a farmer, I want to record my harvest with quantity and quality,
so that I have accurate yield data.
- Points: 5 | Priority: CRITICAL

### US-017: Yield Analysis
As a farmer, I want to see my yield per hectare compared to expected,
so that I know how productive my farm is.
- Points: 5 | Priority: HIGH

### US-018: Farm Calendar
As a farmer, I want a calendar view of all farm activities,
so that I can plan and track upcoming tasks.
- Points: 8 | Priority: MEDIUM

### US-019: Weather Information
As a farmer, I want to see weather forecasts for my farm location,
so that I can plan activities around weather conditions.
- Points: 8 | Priority: MEDIUM

### US-020: Pest & Disease Recording
As a farmer, I want to record pest and disease occurrences with photos,
so that I can track issues and get recommendations.
- Points: 8 | Priority: HIGH

## Epic 5: Finance

### US-033: Expense Recording
As a farmer, I want to record all farm expenses with receipts,
so that I know where my money goes.
- Points: 5 | Priority: CRITICAL

### US-034: Income Recording
As a farmer, I want to record all farm income,
so that I can track my earnings.
- Points: 5 | Priority: CRITICAL

### US-035: Profit & Loss View
As a farm owner, I want to see profit and loss statements,
so that I know if my farm is profitable.
- Points: 8 | Priority: HIGH

### US-036: Budget Management
As a farm manager, I want to create budgets and compare with actuals,
so that I can control costs.
- Points: 8 | Priority: HIGH

### US-037: Cost Per Crop Analysis
As a farm owner, I want to see cost and profit per crop,

- Points: 5 | Priority: HIGH

### US-040: List Produce for Sale

### US-041: Browse and Search Produce

### US-042: Make Purchase Offer

- Points: 5 | Priority: HIGH

### US-046: Rating and Reviews

- Points: 3 | Priority: MEDIUM

### US-047: Price Trends

- Points: 5 | Priority: MEDIUM

### US-048: Buyer-Seller Messaging

## Epic 8: Offline & Sync

### US-053: Work Offline
As a farmer in a remote area, I want to record activities without internet,
so that I can work anywhere.
- Points: 13 | Priority: HIGH

### US-054: Automatic Sync
As a farmer, I want my offline records to sync automatically,
so that I don't have to manually upload data.
- Points: 13 | Priority: HIGH

### US-055: Sync Status Visibility
As a user, I want to see what has and hasn't been synced,
so that I know my data is safely on the server.
- Points: 5 | Priority: MEDIUM
